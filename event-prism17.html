PRISM#17 NEUESTE MUSIK TÜRKISCHER KOMPONIST*INNEN, JOURNEY OF SOUND

Sunday, May 18, 2025 11:30 AM WUK, Wien

The ensemble airborne extended presents the newest works by young Turkish composers in Vienna, which were premiered at the Journey of Sound Festival in Istanbul in early May 2025, showcasing excerpts from a diverse contemporary Turkish music landscape: including music by Onur Dülger, Mehmet Ali Uzunselvi, Uğurcan Öztekin, Didem Coşkunseven (Austrian Premiere), Azra Kaya (Austrian Premiere), Yunus Eren Kırmızıgül (Austrian Premiere), Toykan Efe Kahraman (Austrian Premiere), Orkay Pazarcı (Austrian Premiere) and Egemen Kurt (World Premiere).

Matinee concert with Turkish coffee and desserts.
